# Viral push — bracket posts

Sequence: create Threads account → post your own bracket → Reddit → Twitter. Same picture everywhere (share card PNG), slightly different words. Post while quarterfinals are still open, dies after semis.

Handle: @pickthecup. Bio: "Calling the whole World Cup before it happens. Prove me wrong." Pin the first bracket post.

## 3. Reddit (r/worldcup, r/soccer daily thread, r/FIFAWorldCup)

Don't lead with the link, Reddit smells promo instantly. Post the card image with a hot take as the title, link in a comment when someone asks.

>

>
> do yours and quote me with it. i'll check back after the final: pick-the-cup.vercel.app

## Rules of thumb

- Card image in every post. The bracket is the content, the app is a footnote.
- One spicy pick per post. "Argentina wins" is boring, "France out in the semi" starts fights.
- Reply to every quote/comment with your own bracket comparison. Fights = reach.
- Repost the moment a real result confirms or kills one of your picks. "Called it" and "well, that's my bracket dead" both work.
