# Creator outreach kit (2026-07-02)

Operational kit for the zero-budget creator push in [00-executive-plan.md](00-executive-plan.md) and [04-viral-growth-tactics.md](04-viral-growth-tactics.md). Everything referenced here is **built and live** as of July 2: referral links, creator-branded share cards, and the private performance report.

**The clock:** Round of 16 starts July 7, Final is July 19. A creator recruited today gets two full rounds of content out of the tournament; one recruited after July 11 gets scraps. Front-load the DMs.

## 1. What a creator gets (the offer)

No cash upfront — the offer is infrastructure + status + a bonus that scales with proof:

1. **A personal link** — `https://pick-the-cup.vercel.app/?ref=THEIRHANDLE`. Every visitor who lands on it is credited to them, and stays credited (first-touch) even if they come back later or through someone else's share.
2. **Their handle on the viral artifact** — every share card generated by people they brought in carries a gold "🤝 with @theirhandle" badge, and every re-share keeps crediting them. Their brand rides the chain, not just the first click.
3. **Founding-creator framing** — they're one of a handful of creators on the tool for the tournament, not one of ten thousand affiliates. Early access to anything shipped next (private leagues, premium features).
4. **A bonus tied to real numbers, not vibes** — see §5. Frame as bonus, never as the headline (the headline is the content opportunity: the format already trends, the tool is the demo).

## 2. Link mechanics (what to tell creators — and what's true)

- Landing on `?ref=handle` is counted server-side per day (`ref_visits` table).
- If that visitor saves a bracket (email capture), the save is stamped with the creator's handle — that's the **conversion** metric.
- The handle must be a lowercase slug (`a-z 0-9 _ -`, max 32 chars) — pick it for them in the DM so there's zero setup friction on their side.
- Attribution survives the viral chain: shares from their referred users carry `ref=` + `by=` onward.

## 3. Who to target and where to find them

**Profile (from 04):** football content creators, **10K–100K followers**, already posting predictions/reactions (not general lifestyle), US-based preferred (host nation, best CPMs, tightened geo-authentication rewards authentic US activity). Nano/micro tier gets comparable FYP reach to macro accounts — highest ROI per DM.

**Where to look, in order of yield:**
- TikTok: search/browse `#WorldCup2026`, `#WorldCupBracket`, `#FIFA2026`, "AI predicts World Cup" — target accounts doing the *predict/react* formats, they need exactly this tool.
- YouTube Shorts: search "World Cup 2026 predictions", "World Cup bracket" — filter to channels 10K–100K subs with uploads in the last week.
- Threads/X: accounts live-posting match reactions with real engagement (replies, not just likes).
- Fiverr sports-UGC filter — the *paid* fallback ($50–150/video) for guaranteed volume; keep separate from the free founding-creator track.

**Disqualify:** accounts that post scripted ads back-to-back (their audience is dead), betting-tip accounts (see the legal callout in 03 — do not co-brand with betting content).

## 4. Message templates

Rules: personalize the first line with their actual recent post, keep DMs under 4 sentences, ask for a *duet-style/predict-with-me* post — never a scripted ad. American spelling.

### 4d. Content brief (send after a yes)

> Format that works: film yourself filling the bracket (screen-record or over-the-shoulder), react to your own picks, end on the champion card. Post it as "predict with me — duet/stitch with your bracket." Put your link in bio + pinned comment.
> Two honesty rules that protect your reach: don't frame it as "AI knows who wins" (TikTok reach-limits misleading AI framing), and keep title/thumbnail matched to what's in the video (YouTube's clickbait model throttles mismatches). "I predicted all 64 games — watch it age badly" is exactly right.

## 5. Tracking, bonuses, payout math

**Check numbers** (auth = same secret as the results cron, in Vercel env):

```bash
curl -H "Authorization: Bearer $CRON_SECRET" https://pick-the-cup.vercel.app/api/referrals
# → [{ "ref": "theirhandle", "visits": 412, "saved": 37 }, ...]
```

- **Judge on `saved` (brackets with email), not `visits`** — visits are inflatable, saved brackets are real users.
- Suggested bonus ladder (only promise after their first post is live): $10 at 25 saved brackets, $25 at 100, $100 at 500, paid via PayPal/Venmo. Cheap, capped, milestone-based — no open-ended rev-share promises (per 03, there's no revenue to share yet; sponsor money comes *after* traffic exists).
- Share the creator's own numbers with them proactively at each round boundary (July 7, 11, 15) — it's the retention mechanic for the *creator*, same way the leaderboard is for players.

## 7. Daily ops (10 min/day)

1. DM 5 new creators (batch-find 15–20 once, drip the DMs).
2. Bump yesterday's non-responders once; drop after the bump.
3. `curl /api/referrals` — update the tracker, message any creator who crossed a milestone.
4. When a creator's post shows organic traction in its first 48h, that's the Spark-Ads boost candidate (see 04 §3) — don't boost cold posts.
