# Instagram

Stories are the play — card image + sticker. Feed only for milestones.

## Story 1 — bracket lock

Card image full screen. Poll sticker on the champion: "[Team] wins it all — genius / delusional". Link sticker: pick-the-cup.vercel.app, text "make yours, 1 min".

## Story 2 — day of match

Card cropped to the relevant matchup. Countdown sticker to kickoff. Text: "my bracket needs [team] today".

## Story 3 — result reaction

Card with the dead/alive pick circled (draw on it). Text: "called it" or "well." Nothing else — understatement reads better than paragraphs.
